Long Scrolling Menu

  • This demo uses theme greengray.
  • When a long sub-menu has more items than can be displayed in a page, hidden items can be viewed by moving the mouse to up arrow at the top of sub-menu or down arrow at the bottom of sub-menu.
  • See an example under Products > Menu.
